Request Data Deletion

All StreakFast data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data.

How to delete your data

  1. In the App: Open Settings → scroll to "Delete My Data" → confirm to remove all fasting records, weight logs, water logs, and profile data.
  2. Uninstall the App: All data is removed when you uninstall StreakFast.
  3. Contact us: Email clustlestudio@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and we will respond within 48 hours.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
